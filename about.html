About Me

Hello there! I'm Nikola Skibicka, a dedicated and passionate individual on a mission to turn my love for tattoo art into a lifelong career. Ever since I can remember, I've been fascinated by the intricate details and profound stories behind tattoos. What started as a mere interest has now blossomed into an unwavering commitment to pursue my dream job as a tattoo artist.

For me, tattooing is more than just a profession; it's a form of self-expression, a way to connect with people on a deeper level.

Currently, I am actively seeking a tattoo apprenticeship. I understand that this journey requires dedication, hard work, and a willingness to learn from experienced artists. I am ready to immerse myself fully in the world of tattooing, soaking up knowledge and putting in the hours to refine my technique.

I am excited about the prospect of embarking on this journey towards becoming a professional tattoo artist.
